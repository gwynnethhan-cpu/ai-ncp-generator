Data Privacy Notice

Aligned with the Data Privacy Act of 2012

Collection of data: This system collects patient demographic data, admitting diagnosis, chief complaint, assessment findings, vital signs, medication information, and nursing care plan entries for documentation, educational, and clinical planning purposes.

Use of data: The collected data will be used only for generating, reviewing, and documenting a nursing care plan within the scope of the approved project or clinical activity.

Retention period: Encoded data will be retained only for as long as necessary for the academic, clinical, or project purpose for which it was collected, and shall not be kept longer than necessary.

Disposal of data: After the retention period or once the documentation purpose has been completed, digital records shall be securely deleted and printed copies, if any, shall be shredded or disposed of using secure destruction methods.

Confidentiality and legal basis: Access must be limited to authorized users only. All processing of information must observe confidentiality and the principles of the Data Privacy Act of 2012, including lawful processing, proportionality, transparency, and secure disposal of records.

AI notice: The AI component is supportive only. Final nursing judgment, validation, and documentation remain the responsibility of the nurse.
